# Welcome to Biatec CLAMM

**Biatec CLAMM** is a next-generation **Concentrated Liquidity Automated Market Maker (CLAMM)** built on the **Algorand blockchain**. Experience efficient, capital-optimized decentralized trading with advanced features designed for both retail users and sophisticated DeFi protocols.

## üöÄ What is Biatec CLAMM? {#--what-is-biatec-clamm}

Biatec CLAMM revolutionizes decentralized exchange (DEX) functionality by implementing **concentrated liquidity** - a breakthrough that allows liquidity providers to allocate their capital within specific price ranges, dramatically improving capital efficiency and reducing impermanent loss compared to traditional AMMs.
